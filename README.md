Welcome to my portfolio repository! This repository serves as a showcase of my work, skills, and accomplishments as digital and analog circuits designer, semiconductor and hardware security professional. Here, you'll find information about me, the projects I've worked on, and how to get in touch.

## About Me
I am Crisphine Ngari a MSc student at Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in Southern Illinois University Carbondale.
I design circuits and study the systems they power. My work spans analog and mixed-signal design, digital VLSI, and wireless communication, with a growing focus on the security of IoT hardware and machine learning. I love to create solutions that make a difference.
I am advised by Prof. Ning Weng .
